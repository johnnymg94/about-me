My Name Is Johnny

Yep, that’s me. You’re probably wondering how I ended up in this situation.

I was born on April 8th 1994 in town called Liverpool. I grew up in an area called Bootle and if you’ve never been then that’s defiantly something you should stick too. By age 11 I moved to a place called Netherton and if you haven’t been there either then keep continuing with your sensible life decisions.

Speaking of sensible life decisions I haven’t made many. When I was 6 I was sat down by my friends in the school canteen and told to pick a football team; Liverpool or Everton. I didn’t know much about them both so I just chose Everton. I’ve thought about that moment a lot through the years.

I inherited the bad decision gene from my parents. In total I have a family of 4 brothers and 3 sisters so Christmas is an expensive time of year and is probably why I don’t won’t to add children of my own anytime soon.

I graduated from Edge Hill University in 2015 with a degree in Film & Television which, at the time, I though was something of some use. Since then I’ve went on to have a career in directing, photography, graphic design, motion graphics, creative management, event producing and a visual technician all the while being colour blind.

I have a list of things I’d like to learn and pursue in my life such as creative directing, stage design, creating visual exhibitions and of course coding. I’m just trying to be a creative person, my name is Earl… I mean Johnny.
